Program description

The GREYS Staff Scheduling Notifications program sends work-related text messages to employees of Greys Fine Cheese & Entertaining who opt in through the staff portal: published work schedules and schedule changes, reminders the day before a scheduled shift, time-off request decisions, shift swap updates, and team announcements from a manager.

Opt-in

Employees opt in by signing into the staff portal at staff.greyscheese.com/schedule.html with their employee ID and entering their mobile number under Notifications. Consent is optional and is not a condition of employment.

Message frequency

Message frequency varies with scheduling activity — typically a few messages per week (a weekly schedule text, shift reminders on days before you work, and occasional announcements).

Fees

Message and data rates may apply according to your mobile carrier plan. GREYS does not charge for these messages.

Opt-out and help

Reply STOP to any message to cancel and stop receiving texts. You can also remove your mobile number on the schedule page of the staff portal, or ask a manager to remove it. Reply HELP for help, or contact us at info@greyscheese.com.

Support

For questions about the program, contact info@greyscheese.com or speak with your manager. Carriers are not liable for delayed or undelivered messages.

Privacy

Personal information is handled as described in our Privacy Policy. Mobile numbers and opt-in data are never sold or shared with third parties for marketing.
